About Us

Dream Diagnostics Center is a hi-tech diagnostic center of international standards in Mangalore, committed to delivering reliable, fast, accurate, and affordable diagnostic services with a focus on quality patient care since 2025.

Our Mission

To provide accurate and timely diagnostic, prognostic, and therapeutic investigation reports at an affordable rate to every customer, enabling effective patient treatment.

Our Vision

To serve everyone willingly and help fight the battle against disease by providing world-class diagnostic healthcare and setting high standards for patient care.

Our Core Values

We are committed to seamless and simplified diagnostic services driven by technology and a customer-first approach. We prioritize quality testing and reporting at affordable prices.
